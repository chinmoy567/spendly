Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data Collection

Spendly collects information necessary to provide and improve our personal finance tracking service. When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Account login credentials (securely hashed)
  • Expense data you enter (amounts, categories, dates, descriptions)
  • Usage analytics and interaction data
  • Device information and IP address for security purposes

We do not collect sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers or payment method details. All data collection is transparent, and you can review what information we have about you at any time.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Spendly service
  • To authenticate your account and prevent unauthorized access
  • To send important account notifications and updates
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our features
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Restricted access to personal data by authorized personnel only
  • Secure password hashing using modern cryptographic algorithms

While we strive to protect your information, no security system is completely impenetrable. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

4. User Rights and Control

You have the right to control your personal data. Specifically, you can:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates to your information
  • Export your expense data in a standard format
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Spendly uses cookies to enhance your user experience. These cookies help us:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze how you use our service to improve functionality
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. If you disable cookies, some features of Spendly may not function properly. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

Spendly may integrate with third-party services to enhance functionality. When we do, we ensure these partners maintain similar privacy standards. We never share your personal data with third parties without your consent, except:

  • When required by law or court order
  • To protect against fraud or security threats
  • As necessary to provide the services you request

Each third-party integration is clearly disclosed, and you can opt-out of any integration at any time.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove all personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law. Expense data associated with a deleted account is permanently erased and cannot be recovered.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, by sending you an email notification.

Your continued use of Spendly after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app
